What is Careyes?

Welcome to Casa Torre

Situated upon dramatic cliffs above the emerald waters of the Pacific Ocean in Careyes, Mexico, Casa Torre boasts a magnificent view of the beach below, Playa Rosa, Teopa Beach and others beyond.

Designed by Diego Villaseñor, the house is comprised of a series of tropical thatched roof palapas and open living areas that offer cliff-side views of the ocean. The main palapa, with a ceiling height of over 7 meters contains the open-air living and dining area while ancillary palapas contain five very private bedrooms, each with a king bed, ensuite bath and terrace or balcony.

Casa Torre is known for it's peerless service and exquisite food and drink. Our professional staff will greet you upon arrival, and attend to you and your guests, while respecting your privacy. You will be served local fare that utilizes only the freshest ingredients.

What is Careyes?

Careyes, which translates into 'tortoise,' can only be described as a piece of paradise conceived and meticulously developed by Italian entrepreneur Gian Franco Brignone. Home to hundreds of species of birds and a nesting ground for the native sea turtle, Careyes is secluded within a protected biosphere that stretches along the wild Pacific coast of Mexico.

Private beaches for sun bathing, boating, spear fishing, fishing for dorado marlin, sailfish and tuna; horseback and polo; and whale watching are just part of the allure of the stunning coastline. The Cuixmala Preserve and the Brignone Foundation ensure that Careyes will remain one of the most protected enclaves in Mexico.

Amenities

Located in a private gated community, Casa Torre has 5 spacious bedrooms, each with king bed, bath, and air conditioning. High-speed internet (Wi-Fi) is available in the main Palapa. An infinity swimming pool has a separate bath, changing room and shower. Massage, manicure, yoga, shaman, and therapist are available by appointment. Daily laundry service is provided.

A 24 ft. Boston Whaler, 'The Medusa,' is anchored below the house in front of Playa Rosa, with captain, and is available for use for outings to beaches and bird preserves, or for fishing and picnics.

The nearest Cuixmala Ecological Preserve can be toured by appointment to learn more about the surrounding unique biosphere that created a "dry jungle." Local wildlife includes the Frigate bird, Roseate Spoonbill, brown Pelican, Coatimundi, wild boar, jaguars, and sea turtles.

Where Are We?

Casa Torre is located in Careyes, 1 hour 15 minutes to the north of the Manzinillo International Airport and 2 hours 30 minutes to the South of the Puerto Vallarta Airport. Private airplanes can be chartered between the Puerto Vallarta Airport and a 4,000 ft. grass landing strip near Careyes, in Chamela. Casa Torre offers transportation to and from the Manzinillo Internation Airport, and luxury car service and taxis are also available at both airports.
